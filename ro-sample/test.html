Abstract

\boldmath Computational scientific workflows provide a means for modeling, executing and disseminating computational experiments. These workflows usually include several dependencies as part of their definition, either as data dependencies or as infrastructure dependencies. In this paper we propose a lightweight ontology network that captures the infrastructure dependencies, and we combine it with the semantic descriptions of a catalog of workflows to repair their broken steps and fragments. We validate our approach with a set of real world workflows in the Text Analytics domain, and discuss the extensibility of our approach to other infrastructures and domains.
